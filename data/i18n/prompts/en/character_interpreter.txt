classify it into a structured action type, extract the relevant parameters,
and flag any ambiguities.

move, talk, train_stat, train_technique, use_technique, fight, spy, steal, buy, sell,
work, rest, meditate, research, declare_goal, request_objective_path,
pay_for_information, accept_mission, submit_mission, challenge, seduce, intimidate,
bribe, pray, wait, custom.

RULES:
- If the action is clear, fill in all fields.
- If it is ambiguous, fill clarification_questions with 1 or 2 short questions.
- target_id is the canonical id of a character, location, or technique if applicable.

You always respond in JSON conforming to the schema.
